Short answer: West Peek Productions is a virtual and hybrid event production company that combines strategy, technology support, and calm execution so events feel legitimate and run cleanly.

What this is not: This is not a tool directory or a template library. It’s an operator-led production service designed to remove risk, uncertainty, and last-minute chaos.

Decision thresholds: If the event has external audiences, executives, sponsors, or reputational risk, production quality is not optional. If your team can’t rehearse end-to-end, bringing in an experienced operator is often the safest move.

Talk to me like an executive

If I were advising a leader, I’d treat the event like a public credibility moment: choose the simplest platform that meets constraints, then invest in rehearsal, clear ownership, and a calm control loop. The audience should never feel the backstage.

What we would not recommend

We would not recommend platform shopping as a substitute for production. We would not recommend going live without rehearsal. We would not recommend adding complexity the team can’t run under pressure.

About West Peek Productions

West Peek Productions is a specialized production company founded in 2020 by Scooter Taylor (Class of ’11). Our work is simple to describe and hard to do well: a turnkey event management process with high-quality technical expertise, creative content, seamless AV production, and calm, flawless execution.

We have produced 400+ virtual and hybrid events, including conferences, summits, galas, pitch competitions, internal board meetings, career fairs, college homecomings, and more.

Who we’re built to serve

  • Marketing & communications leaders
  • Event professionals
  • Heads of community for cross-functional organizations
  • Foundations, nonprofits, associations, and educational institutions
  • Specialized teams inside Fortune 500 organizations (HR, Sales, Marketing, etc.)

Here’s how it works

Technology management before the event. We guide, support, and optimize the setup, including a beforehand tech check to ensure the best experience for attendees and participants.

Production services during the event. We handle visuals, graphics, and overlays; utilize professional-grade cameras and audio equipment to deliver crystal-clear video and sound quality; and can support advanced capabilities like 3D/VR when it’s appropriate for the experience.

Speaker support after the event. VIP service: we handle logistics, details, and run-throughs so speakers show up prepared and the team isn’t carrying hidden risk.
